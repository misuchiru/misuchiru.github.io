Privacy Policy for SudoMind

Last updated: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application SudoMind (“the App”, “we”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects user information. We are committed to respecting your privacy and complying with applicable laws.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

However, the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain non-personal information, including:

  • Device information (e.g., model, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / AAID)
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Approximate location (not GPS-level precision)

This data is processed by Google according to its own privacy policy. We do not have access to information that can identify you personally.

2. How We Use Information

  • Delivering and optimizing advertisements
  • Analyzing ad performance and app usage (via Google services)

The App does NOT:

  • Collect personal data
  • Require account creation
  • Sell, rent, or share your data with third parties (other than AdMob)
  • Store any user information on our servers

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
Google Privacy Policy

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages (4+).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through Google’s ad services, please contact us and we will assist you as needed.

5. Data Security

We take privacy seriously. Because the App does not collect or store personal information, no sensitive data is retained on our systems.

6. Your Rights

Since the App does not collect any personal information, users do not need to request access, correction, or deletion of data.
For Google AdMob–related data preferences, please refer to Google’s privacy controls.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the App signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
